Who are we?

We are Blood and Brass Productions. We are a small team of indie game developers working on making the best games we can. Following this mantra we are now working on our first project "Rustharte."

Games

Rustharte

Rustharte is a game where you survive wave after wave of monsters in the wasteland. There are many diffrent modes you can play in like last stand. Where you must withstand an assault on a bridge in a last stand. In stronghold you must survive and defend the stronghold. If the stronghold gets destroyed it's gameover. Multiplayer might be added.
